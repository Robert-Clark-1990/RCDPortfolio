The final page in my portfolio is another passion project of mine. Alongside my coding and artwork, I am also a self-published author. I wanted to create a site that would match the quality and design of other prolific crime and thriller authors. The focus of this page is to encourage sign ups to my mailing list through the use of a free book modal that appears when the user first visits the page. They will have the choice to join this list, or continue on to discover my other works, learn more about me as an author, or view some frequently asked questions.

This project is currently ongoing and will likely change before deployment. The current live page link will direct you to my Wordpress site (also designed by me before I learned to code). Information on features, technologies used, testing and deployment can be found on the project's GitHub page.
