My first milestone project in my Diploma in Software Development, and first foray into coding was the creation of a website for comedian, podcaster, screenwriter, and wife of me, Milly Armstrong Clark. The design features a fun and colourful aesthetic in keeping with Milly's comedic style, and showcases a selection of her work, a news page, and a contact form.

This project was built using the five planes of UX design to develop a site that is focused in its needs and objectives, and provides a high quality experience for the user. All assets used were created by me. I began development on this website less than a month after beginning my diploma in Software Development with just HTML and CSS.

Information on features, testing and deployment can be found on the project's GitHub page.
